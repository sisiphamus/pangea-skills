--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
  THE OATH OF THE PACK
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

     1. Skills are markdown. Not a product. Not a daemon.
     2. Portable paths only. No /home/someone/secretvault.
     3. purpose: in frontmatter or it is fog, not a land.
     4. share: public means it may leave the mountain.
     5. Quality over inventory. Empty shelves beat bad coasts.
